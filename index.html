Game Modes

Each version offers multiple game modes to satisfy different player needs and preferences. From beginners to experts, there's a mode for everyone!

Learn Mode

Learn Mode allows you to visualize the behaviors of the ghosts. Colored squares represent the ghost targets, helping you understand how ghost AI works and improve your gameplay strategy.

Practice Mode

This mode provides special features like slow-motion, time rewinding, invincibility, and ghost behavior visualization. Perfect for players who want to master the game, study its mechanics, and improve their skills.

Turbo Mode

A popular hardware modification found in original arcade cabinets. In this mode, Pac-Man moves twice as fast (matching the speed of ghost eyes) and doesn't slow down when eating pellets. Perfect for players seeking a greater challenge!

Controls

Pac-Man controls are simple and intuitive, suitable for players of all ages. Below are the main controls and additional operations for special modes.

Main Controls

  • Swipe: Steer Pac-Man on mobile devices
  • Arrow Keys: Control Pac-Man movement
  • End Key: Pause the game
  • Esc Key: Open in-game menu

Practice Mode Extra Controls

  • Shift: Hold down to rewind time (like in Braid)
  • 1: Hold to slow game speed to 0.5x
  • 2: Hold to slow game speed to 0.25x
  • o: Toggle Pac-Man Turbo mode
  • i: Toggle Pac-Man invincibility
  • q,w,e,r,t: Toggle target graphics for Blinky, Pinky, Inky, Clyde, and Pac-Man
